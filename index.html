Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How often should I replace my RO filters?

Generally, pre-filters should be replaced every 6-8 months, RO membrane every 2-3 years, and post-carbon filter every 12 months. However, this depends on water quality and usage. Our AMC service includes regular filter replacements at optimal intervals.

What is included in your AMC service?

Our AMC includes regular maintenance visits, filter replacements, system cleaning, performance check, water quality testing, and priority support. We offer both basic and premium AMC plans to suit different needs and budgets.

How long does installation take?

Standard RO installation typically takes 2-3 hours. This includes site assessment, mounting the unit, connecting plumbing, testing the system, and customer training. Commercial installations may take longer depending on system complexity.

Do you provide warranty on your products?

Yes, all our RO systems come with manufacturer warranty. Typically 1-2 years for domestic units and up to 3 years for commercial systems. Installation and service warranty is also provided separately for added peace of mind.

Can you service other brand RO systems?

Yes, our expert technicians can service and maintain most RO system brands. We stock genuine spare parts for popular brands and can provide AMC services for existing installations as well.

How do I know if my water needs RO purification?

We provide free water testing service to check TDS levels, pH balance, and contamination. If TDS is above 300 ppm or if there are bacterial/chemical contaminants, RO purification is recommended. Our experts will guide you on the best solution.
